multiculture museum

#Branding   #Graphics

For my branding class assignment (2014), I designed branding for Multiculture Museum located in Seoul, Korea. People could enjoy miniature landmarks, currency, and traditional costumes there. It provided visitors with a variety of multicultural experiences and the feeling of traveling all around the world. The regular hexagon is geometrically stable and easy to expand, as seen in nature, like a honeycomb. I thought it was similar to a globe where each country communicates and expands its culture. The CMYK colour model and connections between each vertex represent the diversity of cultures intricately intertwined.
